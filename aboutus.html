Farra

Farra is the co-founder and lead instructor at Oasis Yoga. She has a deep thirst and passion for movement and continuing education. She is an accredited Yoga Instructor, Personal Trainer and Corrective Exercise Specialist with over 15 years of teaching experience to all ages and levels. She has in-depth knowledge and understanding of the science of human anatomy and biomechanics. She believes that movement is medicine and is essential to our mental, physical, emotional and spiritual well being.

She is an explorer at heart and has spent the last 20 years living and traveling the world to seek out the very best elements of different cultures, philosophies and traditions and continues to be fascinated by the amazing variety of human knowledge and experience. She is a lover of love and believer of flow state. While she takes her job in Wellness very seriously, she doesn’t take herself seriously at all as she knows full well that a lot of unnecessary anxiety, fear and self judgement can simply disappear when things are approached with a light hearted attitude and good sense of humour. Her classes are entirely non dogmatic, whole heartedly accepting and fully inclusive to all.

Kate

Kate is the co-founder of Oasis Yoga, creator of The Oasis Kitchen and Bar and a true jack of all trades. She is the literal engine that keeps the whole thing running as she takes care of branding, marketing, web design and development, social media content, and hotel bookings. She even manages to teach yoga for kids and lead mediation practices. Though her “real job” and education lies in professional photography which, along with travel, remains her deepest passion. She began traveling internationally for humanitarian aid at a very young age and has yet to stop. She has fallen in love with people, places, and experiences from Haiti to Iceland and everything in between. She believes in “the middle way” approach to life. Good luck trying to put her into one box. She is a mediative martial artist, an incredibly serious jokester and the hardest working slacker around. She is as hard as she is soft and always, always up for a good laugh.

Nok

Nook is the Manager at Oasis Yoga Bungalows. She is the solver of all problems and almost always manages to smile while doing so. Work and hobbies often blend as she genuinely enjoys gardening, helping people, social welfare projects and all things artistic. Nook is a small person with a huge heart!!! She has recently undertaken a massive self-initiated project to keep Lanta clean while providing education and jobs to local people in need through teaching the joys of turning trash into treasures. Nok is well known and respected throughout the entire province for her passion and dedication to Eco Conscientious education and action. Follow her efforts and she what she accomplishes next.

Mango

Mango is the head of hospitality and the CEO of cuddles at Oasis Yoga. She takes her responsibility as quality control manager at The Oasis Kitchen very seriously and never takes eyes off the chef. She is the most lovable member of staff and by far the most photogenic. She enjoys short walks on the beach, air conditioned rooms, tuk tuk rides, pretending to be a person, and every kind of food except vegetables.

Sadly, Mango retired June 25th, 2023. We miss her everyday.
