Oasis Yoga Bungalows is a boutique hotel with holistic health in mind, designed to make it simple and easy for guests to enjoy the process of caring for their health while on a relaxing holiday. It’s healthy food that actually tastes good and highly professional yoga classes that are actually fun. We are Eco-Conscience and take great care to keep this stunning island clean and sustainable. We compost, re-use, re-purpose, or recycle 100% of waste so rest assured your only ‘footprints’ are left in the sand. Our vibes are casual and friendly so it’s an incredibly easy going place to make yourself at home right away. Whether you’re new to healthy living or a seasoned sage, Oasis Yoga welcomes you as you are!
