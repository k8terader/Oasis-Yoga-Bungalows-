Pool Suite Bungalow

Three of our pool bungalows have been recently renovated and updated. They have one queen bed, a small twin bed suitable for a third person or a great spot to use like a couch.

These bungalows sit looking onto the shady hammock lounge which is a sweet little spot just off of the pool, making them the quieter option

All About Our Bungalows

These charming, 19m2 stand alone bungalows with cozy front porches are ready for you to sit back and chill out. The rooms are newly and mindfully renovated with your holistic health in mind. We wanted to create a space that is clean, simple and comfortable. Each has their own private bathroom with hot and cold water along with access to clean drinking water and free reusable water bottles to use throughout your stay. We hope you find it easy to be mindful of your resources while you are here and still enjoy the creature comforts these bungalows provide. Each room has an A/C unit and a fan if you to open the windows and enjoy the breeze. We keep the property safe and guarded, but just in case, we also provide in room safety boxes for your reassurance. Each unit has a small fridge, kettle with tea & coffee, and high speed fiber optic wifi. There is no TV however, as we figured with all the beach, yoga, dining and adventure you wouldn't even notice.
