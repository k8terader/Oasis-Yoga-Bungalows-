Our menu is designed around local produce and ingredients with an emphasis on health and sustainability.


Koh Lanta is home to some of the best Thai food in Southern Thailand. At The Oasis Kitchen we aim to use the same local ingredients in a different way. From Spinach Salads to Fish Tacos and fusion Thai,  we have mindfully designed this menu to include something for everyone.

Our Bar offers cold pressed juices made daily from local produce and refreshing smoothies that are loaded with supplements and superfoods. At the bar, we offer signature cocktails, craft beers, and reasonably good wine. Our goal is to allow you to enjoy eating like you're on holiday without going home feeling like you need to recover from holiday.
