All About Our Villas

We call these Villa Bungalows, "our little houses" and we'd love for you to call them home during your stay on Lanta. Both of our Villas have recently undergone a full transformation remodel and now offer an open floor plan kitchen and living room. The kitchen is modern, clean and fully furnished. The living room is cozy and spacious with a super comfy L shaped sofa that can be converted to an extra queen sized bed allowing comfortable sleeping for up to six people. The master bedroom has a beautifully remodeled ensuite bathroom, built in closet space, sitting area and desk. The second bedroom is bright and airy with a queen sized bed and small storage area. There is an additional large built in closet and second newly remodeled bathroom just off the living area. Each Villa Bungalow has its own private garden for your family to enjoy. The large front porches with outdoor sofa lounge area are perfect to relax and enjoy nature. There is an outdoor shower, “mud room” space, plenty of storage for towels and toys, and an outdoor BBQ. These modern two bedroom, two bath, with full furnished kitchen units are the perfect choice to have you feeling right at home.
