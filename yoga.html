How does the sign up work?   

All of our yoga classes are held in the tree house studio. High quality mats are provided free of charge. We do not take reservations for these classes. The check in desks opens 25 mins before class begins. We recommend you come early for your first class with us. If you are on the island for a while and know you are coming to a class, you are welcome to come and sign up in advance, in person. We don't take reservations through phone, email or social media.

 

  • Flow

    This class is an active Vinyasa flow, a style that builds strength and stamina as movements link together rhythmically with the breath. It's suitable for multiple levels, including healthy beginners. Options are always given to maintain accessibility for newer students and to challenge those with more experience. This class often involves weight-bearing, so it is not recommended for current wrist injuries. Total beginners who are also elderly are encouraged to try Yin or Yoga Essentials.

  • Yoga Essentials

    This class introduces the foundational elements of yoga in a supportive and informative way. You'll learn how to move safely through common postures while developing awareness of alignment, breath and body mechanics. The pace is steady and mindful, allowing time to explore proper form and ask questions. Whether you're new to yoga, returning after time away or simply looking to refine your practice, this class provides the tools to help you feel grounded, capable and connected in every movement.

  • Yin Yoga

    This soulful, slow, and sensual class is suitable for anyone. The primary focus is to relax the mind, body and nervous system through longer-held postures and conscious breathing. Each pose or stretch lasts several minutes and is supported by props to encourage deep release and relaxation. Yin is highly recommended for anyone under stress, feeling tense or disconnected or simply wishing to unwind and restore balance. Simply put - this class is for everyone!

  • Mobility

    This class blends yoga, functional movement, and mobility training to improve strength, stability, and range of motion while addressing imbalances in the body. We explore how strength and stretch ideally coexist to support healthy, efficient movement. You'll learn to move with greater awareness, build resilience in your tissues and unlock ease in both daily life and your other movement practices. All levels are welcome in this informative and exploratory class.

  • Gentle Morning Stretch

    This simple, soothing class focuses on slow mindful movements, gentle stretching and breath awareness. It is designed to cultivate mind-body connection, improve flexibility and awaken the body in a calm, supportive way. Suitable for all levels, it's a perfect way to begin the day with ease, presence and intention.

Small Group Training Sessions

Our training sessions are limited to 1-4 people per class. Each class is 500B/person. These sessions are not included in yoga packs or monthly passes. The sessions are held in our private gym. The check in desk is open 10 mins before class begins. Reservations should be made in advance via email or directly with the instructor.

 

  • Boxing and Mitt Work with Kate

    Have fun and work hard learning Boxing and Muay Thai technique in this small group training session. Ideal for beginners through intermediate levels who want to go through the fundamentals of striking and footwork. Great for those who would like to join a larger Muay Thai group class on the island, but first need to have a better understanding of proper form to keep your joints safe and get the most out of your training sessions. Each session includes at least 5 rounds of mitt work along with boxing drills and heavy bag work.

     

Community Classes

These classes are always free and everyone is encouraged and invited to attend. No reservations or experience needed. Please be our guest.

 

  • Guided Meditation

    Whether you are an experienced mediator or a total beginner we invite you to join us in practicing Mindfulness. There are many styles of meditation, we primarily practice Anapanasti which means mindfulness through breathing. We may also practice Metta which focuses on love and kindness and at Oasis we always, always practice Gratitude.
